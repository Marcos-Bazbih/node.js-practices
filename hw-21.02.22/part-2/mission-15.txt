Ashdod is the sixth-largest city and the largest port in Israel
accounting for 60% of the country's imported goods. Ashdod is
located in the Southern District of the country, on the Mediterranean
coast where it is situated between Tel Aviv to the north 32 kilometres
(20 miles) away, and Ashkelon to the south 20 km (12 mi) away. Jerusalem
is 53 km (33 mi) to the east. The city is also an important
regional industrial center.
