Tupac Amaru Shakur (born Lesane Parish Crooks,
June 16, 1971 September 13, 1996),
better known by his stage name 2Pac
and later by his alias Makaveli,
was an American rapper and actor.
Considered one of the most influential
rappers of all time, Shakur is among the
best-selling music artists, having sold more
than 75 million records worldwide. Much of Shakur's
music has been noted for addressing contemporary
social issues that plagued inner cities,
and he is considered a symbol of activism against inequality.
